Khalid Javaid
Position:President & CEO

Regarded as a property icon in Faisalabad, Engr. Khalid Javaid brings his extensive professional experience to his role as Senior Business Partner and Chief Executive Officer of 'Top Realtors'. A former Regional General Manager at PTCL Faisalabad, he has held one of the most senior and privileged positions in the Government sector.
In his past work, Engr. Khalid Javaid demonstrated unwavering dedication and commitment to delivering quality. His most notable achievement was the development of a massive residential campus, 'Tech Town'. The project was accomplished under the Telecom Engineers Co-Operative Housing Society Faisalabad, and spanned over 2000 Kanal, featuring state-of-the-art town planning.
Engr. Javaid is known for his complete commitment to quality and brilliance in real estate. His reputation is built not just on his exceptional professional acumen but also his honesty and the positive attitude he brings to every endeavour.
Possibly the only professional in Faisalabad with such high-level qualifications, relevant experience, and top university education in real estate, Engr. Khalid Javaid is undoubtedly a dominant figure in the real estate business. His vision and guidance have been instrumental in establishing 'Top Realtors' as a leading name in the industry.

Waleed Khalid
Position: Director

Waleed Khalid is a dynamic young professional whose multifaceted skills were honed through a decade of diligent work at prestigious institutions in Pakistan and Canada. A visionary at heart, Waleed aspired to transform Pakistan's skyline after completing his Bachelor's degree with specialized honors in Finance and Business Economics from the globally acclaimed York University in Toronto.
York University, one of North America's top business universities, has imbued Waleed with a relentless drive to exceed expectations.
Prior to his current role, he built and managed one of Faisalabad's most successful flour mills, overseeing its operations from inception to full-scale production, and driving its sales through masterful marketing strategies
Waleed also brings to the table a wealth of experience from his time in Canada as a Real Estate professional. Today, Waleed stands as one of the most competent leaders in the real estate industry, poised to continue shaping the sector's landscape.
